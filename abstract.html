ABSTRACT

The primary requisite of fish culture is the availability of land for fish pond and quality fish seeds. Generally, in scientific fish farming number of various sizes of ponds (as stated above) are required for rearing of various stages of fishes :- (1) Nursery pond (2) Rearing pond (3) Stocking pond . This database is going to take the information regarding the types of fishes, how many fishes are sold, how much amount was colleccted , Information about food , information regarding breeding and eggs , salary to the workers. This database is used for the owner to estimate the amout and collect information easily.
